Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how GSTCopilot ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome Extension and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide and improve our services:

  • Email address provided at the time of purchase (for license key delivery)
  • License key activation data (to verify your subscription status)
  • GSTINs entered by you for validation (processed in real-time, not stored)
  • Basic usage analytics (feature usage counts, no personally identifiable information)

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We are committed to your privacy. GSTCopilot does not:

  • Store or log any GSTIN numbers you validate
  • Access, read, or store any data from the web pages you visit
  • Collect your GST portal login credentials or passwords
  • Share or sell your data to any third party
  • Track your browsing history or activity outside the extension

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for:

  • Delivering your license key to your email upon purchase
  • Verifying your subscription status when the extension is activated
  • Providing customer support when you contact us
  • Sending important service updates and announcements

4. Chrome Extension Permissions

GSTCopilot requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab: To read GSTIN numbers visible on the currently active tab
  • scripting: To highlight and annotate GSTIN numbers on web pages
  • storage: To save your license key and settings locally on your device

All data processed by the extension is handled locally on your device or sent directly to our validation API. No data is stored on our servers beyond what is described above.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Razorpay: For payment processing. Your payment details are governed by Razorpay's privacy policy
  • GST Validation API: GSTIN data is sent to our API for real-time validation against government records

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. Your license key is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API.

7. Data Retention

We retain your email address for the duration of your subscription and for up to 12 months thereafter for support purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us at gstcopilot@gmail.com.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email. Continued use of GSTCopilot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at gstcopilot@gmail.com or call us at +91 81163 17662.
